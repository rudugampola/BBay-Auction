Write a standout title

Your title is the first thing people see when they find your listing. It’s the first impression you make on potential buyers, so it’s important to make it count. A good title will help you sell your item faster and for more money. A bad title can make buyers think twice about your listing, or even skip it altogether.

  • Include keywords that describe your item
  • Be specific about the item you’re selling
  • Use a title that will catch people’s attention
  • We’ll recommend search terms that buyers often use, so be sure to add these in the title.
  • Avoid all caps and focus on specific details like brand, model, size, and color.

Take high-quality photos

High-quality photos are the best way to show off your item.

  • Take photos in good lighting, preferably natural light.
  • Take photos from multiple angles.
  • Take photos of any defects or damage.
  • Take photos of the item in action.
  • Take photos of the item in its packaging.
  • Take photos of the item with any accessories.
  • Take photos of the item with any paperwork.
  • Take photos of the item with any receipts.
  • Take photos of the item with any tags.
  • Take photos of the item with any manuals.
  • Take photos of the item with any warranties.
  • Take photos of the item with any certificates.
  • Take photos of the item with any other items that come with it.
  • Snap your items from multiple angles in a well-lit place, and capture any blemishes for transparency.
